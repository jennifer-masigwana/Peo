What Peo Offers

At Peo, we are your go-to resource for turning entrepreneurial dreams into reality. As a comprehensive Business Information Hub (BIH), we offer everything you need to start, grow, and sustain your business. From funding opportunities and expert mentorship to market insights and motivational tools, we’ve curated valuable resources to guide you through every step of your journey. About Us

Mission

We aim to empower young entrepreneurs in Botswana by providing a centralised platform that offers tools, resources, and mentorship necessary to start and grow successful businesses.

Vision

To be the leading platform for youth entrepreneurship in Botswana, fostering a generation of innovative and impactful business leaders.

Core Values

collaboration, empowerment, innovation, accessibility, growth.
